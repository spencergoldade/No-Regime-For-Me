## Voice and tone – No regime for me

**Generic microcopy patterns** (plain language, active voice, error phrasing) are in `.cursor/rules/frontend/ux-writing-and-microcopy.mdc`. This file is for **plugin-specific** voice and tone.

**Goal:** Keep in-plugin copy clear, direct, and human.

### Voice principles

- **Plain language:** Prefer simple words over jargon.
- **Active voice:** “Save changes” instead of “Changes will be saved”.
- **Concrete:** Say exactly what will happen and what users can do next.

### Examples

- Weak: “An error occurred.”
- Strong: “We couldn’t save your changes. Try again in a few seconds.”

- Weak: “Submit”
- Strong: “Save settings”

**Plugin example:** For plugin-specific errors (e.g. saving options), use the same pattern: “We couldn’t update [thing]. [One concrete next step].”

### Tone adjustments

- **Routine actions:** Neutral and concise.
- **Errors:** Calm and specific; avoid blame or panic.
- **High-stakes flows** (if any): Slightly more detailed, with clear reassurance and next steps.
